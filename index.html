Who We Are.

About Us

Drug invent is a medical technology and digital solution company focused on improving medical deliveries and therapeutic outcomes.
We work across the healthcare supply chain with the aim of building resilience using technology to ensure the right medication is always available and accessible at the right quality and quantity. Our objectives is to improve therapeutic outcomes such that patients gets the best and cost-effective therapeutic outcomes from healthcare providers.
Our digital solutions are Good Pharmacy Practice focused. We aim at strengthening supply chain efficiency, eliminating the risk of substandards/counterfeit products and easing the financial cost burden of medications procurement and management on health institutions.

Our Services

The service we offer is specifically designed to meet your needs.

Consignment Stock

Through our consignment stock solutions we take over the burden of inventory management, financing and procurement of medical consumables from healthcare providers letting them channel more of their resources toward patient care.

Cold chain Solution

Our mission is to create sustainable cold chain solutions designed to deliver consistent standardised temperature sensitive medicines like vaccines, medications and laboratory reagents.

Emergency Medication Solution

We believe that every person deserves access to medications such as oral medications, External use injectables, parenterals which they can rely on when they need them. That's why we created Drug Invent as an easy way to get the necessary medication you need when you need it most, without having to worry.

Biomedical Equipment Solution

We provide the best in biochemical equipment like small medical supplies, surgical dressings, disinfectants/anticeptics, sutures, laboratory supplies. We are a leading supplier of biochemical equipment with a wide range of products to assist you in your work. Our main goal is to provide our clients with the best quality products at affordable prices.
